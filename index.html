Our Philosophy

The paramount role of EASTWOODS College is to empower our studentry, through education and training, with a solid foundation of knowledge, skills and moral values necessary in the attainment of their potential enabling them to be competitive, proactive and productive citizens of our country.

                
            Mission

                    To develop and offer high quality technical, 
                health and higher education programs that  would 
                ensure employability and productivity  of young 
                men and women through proper values and principles 
                as a means to achieve success with excellence 
                for a better quality of life.
            
            
           Vision

                A center of excellence in technical, vocational, health 
            and higher education in Bataan, pursuing dynamic
            program offerings paralleled to global standards to 
            uplift the socio-economic growth of the province.

HISTORY of EASTWOODS College of Science and Technology
(formerly EASTWOODS International Institute of Science and Technology)


EASTWOODS International Institute of Science and Technology, was the 3rd institution founded in Bataan by the President Mr. Gregorio M. Sison Jr,
where the first was EASTWOODS Professional College of Science and Technology (formerly SOFTNET College) in Balanga City founded in 1994, and
second was SOFTNET Information Technology Center in Mariveles founded in 1997.

During the first year of operation, it already hit 196 students renting the 3rd and 2nd Floor of the 3-storey Masagana Bldg. On 2007, its second year of
operation, EASTWOODS - Dinalupihan increased the number of enrollees to 434 students and already occupied the whole building.
